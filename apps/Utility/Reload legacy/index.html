What is Reload?

Reload a simple mp3 player

How does it work?

Put your mp3 in:

--Reload/Contents/Music/Album/Your_album_name/Album.mp3 or Reload/Contents/Music/Single/Your_single_name/Single.mp3

You can set your favourits in the main menu by right clicking or left click to play the music
